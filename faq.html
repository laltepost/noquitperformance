The Product

NØ QUIT is a single-ingredient dietary supplement — 2.5mg of phenylcapsaicin per serving (2 capsules of 1.25mg each), designed to support energy metabolism and exercise performance during high-intensity training. 60 capsules per bottle. 30 servings. No stimulants. No artificial additives.
Phenylcapsaicin is a synthetic capsaicinoid — a compound similar to capsaicin (found in chili peppers) but engineered for better bioavailability and a cleaner sensory profile. NØ QUIT uses aXivite®, a patented, standardized form of phenylcapsaicin studied in published exercise research.
No. NØ QUIT is not a pre-workout. It contains no stimulants, no caffeine, and is not designed to produce an acute energy spike. It is a daily capsule designed for consistent daily use — building support over time rather than delivering a short-term effect.
No. NØ QUIT makes no weight-loss or fat-burning claims. While some phenylcapsaicin research observed effects on fat oxidation during exercise, this product is formulated and marketed for exercise performance and recovery support — not body composition changes.
Other Ingredients: Microcrystalline Cellulose (inert filler), Vegetable Capsule (Hypromellose), Magnesium Stearate (vegetable source, flow agent). These are standard, minimal excipients required for capsule manufacturing at a 2.5mg active dose. No artificial colors, no artificial flavors.
Yes. Every batch of NØ QUIT is independently tested by an accredited third-party laboratory for identity, purity, potency, and contaminants. Certificate of Analysis (CoA) available on request. Contact us at support@noquitperformance.com.

Science

Published peer-reviewed research on phenylcapsaicin (aXivite®) has observed improvements in VO₂ max, time-to-exhaustion, and metabolic efficiency during high-intensity exercise in trained athletes. Recovery-related markers also improved in repeat-effort protocols. NØ QUIT is formulated at the dose range studied (2.5mg daily). For more detail, see our Science page.
NØ QUIT is a dietary supplement. Dietary supplement claims are not evaluated by the FDA in the same way as drug claims. All NØ QUIT language uses structure/function claims only — the compound "supports" performance and recovery. This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.
NØ QUIT is manufactured in the United States in a facility operating under cGMP (Current Good Manufacturing Practices) — the FDA's quality standard for dietary supplement production.

Dosing

Take 2 capsules daily with water. On training days, take 30–60 minutes before your session. On rest days, take with your first meal of the day. Do not exceed 2 capsules per 24 hours.
NØ QUIT is not a stimulant — it does not produce an acute effect in the first dose. Most users report noticing effects within 2–4 weeks of consistent daily use. Consistency matters more than timing. Think of it as a daily support tool, not a pre-workout.
NØ QUIT contains no stimulants and is generally compatible with most other supplements. If you are taking any medications, have a medical condition, or are pregnant or nursing, consult your healthcare provider before use.
Yes. Taking NØ QUIT on an empty stomach before training is acceptable. Some users with sensitive stomachs may prefer taking it with a light meal. If you experience any gastric discomfort, take with food.
