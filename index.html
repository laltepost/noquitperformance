Common Questions

Straight answers.

Phenylcapsaicin is a synthetic capsaicinoid — a compound similar to capsaicin (from chili peppers) but designed for better bioavailability and a cleaner sensory profile. Published research has observed effects on exercise performance metrics including time-to-exhaustion and metabolic efficiency during high-intensity training. NØ QUIT uses 2.5mg per serving — consistent with the dose range studied.
Pre-workouts are typically stimulant-heavy powders designed to spike energy acutely before a session. NØ QUIT is a daily capsule — not a pre-workout. There are no stimulants, no artificial flavors, and no crash. It's designed to support performance and recovery as part of consistent daily training, not to create a short-term jolt.
Take 2 capsules daily. On training days, take them 30–60 minutes before your session. On rest days, take them with a meal. Consistency matters more than timing — daily use is how the compound builds its effect over time.
